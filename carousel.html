Mission

We wanted to help women by showing them the diferent types of violence that they could be suffering, plus, we add some special phone numbers that will help them.

Who?

We are just a bunch of middle school girls that are really concerned about the gender inequality problem, that even though a lot of people have tried to end, it still exists.

Why?

We were assigned a task in the workshop of Beautiful Patterns: create a project with an algorithm that will help something in today's society, that is, help solve a problem. Maybe we will not solve it since it is very difficult, but we will try to help society.

GENDER INEQUALITY CONCEPTS

We will define three crucial concepts of gender inequality: Feminism, Gender and Patriarchy.

First concept: Feminism.

Is a doctrine and social movement that requires women the recognition of abilities and rights that have tradicionally been reserved for men.

The second concept, Gender.

It is the set of people or things that have common general characteristics, conceptually, the difference between the sexes doues not imply legal, economic,family or social inequality.

And lastly, the third concept, Patriarchy.

Is the predominance or greater authority of the male in a society or social group.
